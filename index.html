About Me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the GIDP: Statistics & Data Science program at the University of Arizona. I am currently working in the R. Ken Coit College of Pharmacy, supervised by Prof. Hongxu Ding and Prof. Xiaoxiao Sun.

Prior to this, I received my Master's degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and my Bachelor's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research philosophy centers on bridging the gap between AI models and biomedical application, especially for trustworthy models in clinical usage.

Research Interests

  • Medical imaging, VLM, trustworthy model
  • Nonparametric Statistics, linear programming
  • Bioinformatics & Genomics
